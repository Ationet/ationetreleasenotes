# ATIONET Console (Web Portal) - v2.6.7900

## Deployed to PROD (Apr 22, 2025)

### Start Date in Quota Rules
A new field is now available to configure the start date for monthly and yearly quota rules. This allows specifying the day of the month or year on which the rule begins.

This enhancement is particularly useful, as previously monthly quota rules always ran from the first day of the month to the last day of the month, while yearly rules ran from January 1st to December 31st, with no option to modify this behavior.
