# ATIONET Console (Web Portal) - v 2.6.8150

## PROD Deployment (Jul 01, 2026)

### New Features
* Added a "Taxpayer ID" column to the Identifiers report.
* Added "Taxpayer ID", "Street", and "Street 2" columns to the Contract Pricing and Modifiers report.
* Standardized the Contract Pricing and Modifiers report to always display values with 3 decimal places.
* The "Apply Lowest Price at Authorization" option in Company Contracts now also is respected in sale completions according the the price pre-authorized.


### Fixed Issues and Enhancements

The following issues have been resolved:
* Fixed an issue where the Transactions report was generated in an unsorted manner.
* Unknown transactions are now recorded in the Audit Log.
* Fixed an issue where the right side of account statements was occasionally displayed slightly cut off.
* Transaction Type is now displayed in both English and Spanish in Loyalty Transactions exports.
* Fixed incorrect display of the "Ratios" section in Community Configuration.
* Fixed an issue where Loyalty Account was not being reported in the Loyalty Transactions report.
