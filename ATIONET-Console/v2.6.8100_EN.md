# ATIONET Console (Web Portal) - v2.6.8100

## PROD Deployment (Jun 17, 2026)

### Fixed Issues and Improvements

The following issues have been resolved:
- Improved the payment gateway workflow to prevent payments from being processed through an incorrect flow.
- Statements were displayed with the right side cut off.
- Incorrect display of the "Ratios" section in Community Configuration.
- Incorrect display of the "Expiration Information" section in Community Configuration.
- Language inconsistency in the New Password email.
- Loyalty transaction reports did not indicate whether the account belonged to an anonymous user.
- Transaction Type was displayed in both English and Spanish in the Loyalty Transactions export.
