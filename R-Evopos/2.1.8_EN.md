# Revopos - v2.1.8

## Release Date:
- **Verifone T650p:** Apr 20, 2026
- **Newland N910:** Apr 20, 2026
- **Urovo i9000s:** Apr 20, 2026

### Loyalty Discount Community
Starting with this version, the option “Accumulation” has been renamed to “Loyalty”.

Now, when selecting “Loyalty”, the customer identifier is requested and two options are enabled:

- **Accumulate points**: This maintains the current flow without changes.
- **Apply discount**: This allows retrieving and applying discounts for the selected product according to the configured rules.

During batch closures, the discounts applied within the period will also be displayed.

### Improvements in Loyalty Tickets
The following changes have been made to loyalty tickets (point accumulation, balance inquiry, reward redemption):
- The PAN is now masked
- The name of the points is added as if it were the unit
- The customer name is added
- The amount of accumulated points is added
- "Program" has been renamed to "Community"
