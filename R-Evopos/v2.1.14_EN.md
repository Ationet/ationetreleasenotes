# RevoPOS - v2.1.14

## Release Date:
- **Verifone T650p:** TBD
- **Newland N910:** TBD
- **Urovo i9000s:** TBD

### Fusion and Loyalty Integration

Starting with this version, the loyalty workflow, including both points accumulation and discounts, has been incorporated into the terminal when operating in connected mode with Fusion.

### Fixed Issues:

- Quantity set to 0 could not be easily modified.
- Error message was truncated when attempting to change the supervisor password to the current password.
- "Communication Error" occurred when attempting to reverse a loyalty accumulation transaction.
- Discount information was not persisted in Fusion when performing a fleet operation.
- Identity number was not reported in loyalty discount transactions.
- Amount values exceeding 8 digits were displayed incorrectly.
- Renamed "Transaction to Authorize" to "Operation to Authorize" in Gift Card activation.
- Application froze when configuring a fuel/dry product with an invalid amount.
- Loyalty discount receipts displayed "Accumulation" in the title.
- Sales remained blocked in Fusion and were not released.
- Selecting "Back" during a loyalty operation prompted for the identification again instead of returning to the previous screen.
- Dispatches generated from the terminal were recorded as zero-value sales when a dispatch existed under the registered authorization code.
- Balance inquiry was displayed incorrectly when the user had no available balance.
